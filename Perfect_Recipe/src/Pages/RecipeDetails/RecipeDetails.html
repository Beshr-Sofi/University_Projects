This authentic Italian tiramisu is a classic dessert that combines espresso-soaked ladyfingers with a rich, creamy mascarpone filling. Perfect for special occasions or when you want to impress your guests with a taste of Italy.

Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until pale and creamy.
2. Add mascarpone cheese to the egg mixture and beat until well combined.
3. Dip ladyfingers in espresso and layer in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
4. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
5. Repeat layers and dust with cocoa powder. Refrigerate for 4 hours.

Pro Tips

Room Temperature Ingredients

Use mascarpone cheese at room temperature for smoother mixing.

Quick Dipping

Dip ladyfingers quickly to prevent them from getting too soggy.

Chilling Time

Don't skip the chilling time - it allows flavors to develop.
